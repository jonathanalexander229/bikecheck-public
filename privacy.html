Privacy Policy

How BikeCheck protects your data

Last updated: October 27, 2024

Overview

BikeCheck is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our bike maintenance tracking application.

Information We Collect

Strava Integration Data

  • Profile Information: Your Strava athlete ID, name, and profile picture
  • Bike Information: Bike names and IDs from your Strava gear
  • Activity Data: Limited activity data including ride duration, distance, and bike used (last 30 activities only)

App Usage Data

  • Service Intervals: Maintenance schedules and reminders you create
  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device

How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate service intervals based on actual ride time
  • Send maintenance notifications when components need attention
  • Sync your bikes and recent activities from Strava
  • Provide personalized maintenance tracking

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All your data is stored locally on your device using secure system frameworks
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your personal data on external servers
  • Strava OAuth: We use secure OAuth tokens to access your Strava data
  • Limited Scope: We only request essential permissions from Strava

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information to third parties. Your data remains on your device and is only accessed by the BikeCheck app.

Strava Integration

BikeCheck integrates with Strava using their official API. We only access:

  • Basic profile information (name, profile picture)
  • Your bikes/gear list
  • Recent ride activities (last 30 rides of type "Ride" only)

We do not access GPS data, heart rate, power data, or other detailed activity metrics.

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete individual service intervals or clear all data
  • Logout: You can disconnect from Strava at any time
  • Data Export: Your data can be backed up through device backup systems

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as our app evolves. We will notify users of any significant changes through app updates.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through the Support page.
